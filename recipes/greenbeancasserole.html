Green Bean Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 4 quarts water
  • 2 pounds green beans, ends trimmed and halved
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 pound white mushrooms
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced finely
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 5 tablespoons flour
  • 1&1/2 cups chicken broth
  • 1&1/2-2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 container of French Fried Onions

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Fill a large bowl with water, add salt, bring to a boil. Add green beans and cook until bright green and crisp tender (about 6 min).
  3. Run green beans under cold water. Drain well.
  4. Add butter to pot, then melt over medium high heat. Add mushrooms, garlic, salt and pepper. Cook for about 6 min.
  5. Add flour and cook about 1 min.
  6. Stir in broth and bring to a boil, stirring constantly.
  7. Add cream and reduce heat to medium low.
  8. Simmer until sauce thickened (about 15 min). Add salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Add cooked green beans and stir. Spray a 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray. Add green beans to dish.
  10. Bake for 10 min. Remove from oven, add crispy onions, then bake 5-7 more min.
